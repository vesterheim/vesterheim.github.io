This fabulous community event at the Hotel Winneshiek in beautiful downtown Decorah showcases the talents of area residents who create beautiful table settings to spark your holiday decorating ideas and help make your holiday celebrations festive and joyful. 

There are three fantastic events, each with its own unique focus and each featuring the wonderful tables, of course.

Events this year are:
----------------------
**Blue Jeans & Bling Open House**
Friday, Nov. 4 
6:00 - 8:00 p.m. 

**The Luncheon**
(reservations required) 
Saturday, Nov. 5 
12:00 - 2:00 p.m.

**Gala Dinner and Live Auction**
(reservations required) 
Saturday, Nov. 5 
Begins at 5:00 p.m.

More information about each event is coming soon.

View pictures from previous Deck the Tables on the Deck the Tables Facebook page [here.](https://www.facebook.com/Deckthetables/)

Proceeds from Deck the Tables 2016 will be used to support the 4th Grade Pioneer Immersion Program in 2016, providing an invaluable educational partnership between Vesterheim and area schools!
