When Disney Called

Oct 18, 2014

Do you live in the Madison, Wisconsin, area?

Come to the luncheon presentation When Disney Called by Vesterheim staff to learn more about Vesterheim’s connection to the hit movie Frozen. The event will be from 11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. on October 18 at the Craftsman Table and Tap in Madison, Wisconsin.

Tickets are $30 per person. Reservations are required.

For more information and to RSVP, contact Stephanie Johnson at (563) 382-9681, ext.103, or snjohnson@vesterheim.org.
