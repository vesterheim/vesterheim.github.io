The Tours to Norway Coordinator is responsible for designing, implementing, promoting, and evaluating Vesterheim’s yearly tours to Norway. The Tours to Norway Coordinator works with the development team to ensure that all participants in Vesterheim’s tours receive a high quality experience with the institution. 

Reports to 
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

President/CEO and communicates with the development team to increase membership and charitable giving opportunities. 

Minimum Requirements: 
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

* A bachelor’s degree. 

* Exceptional interpersonal and organizational skills and demonstrated experience in working with groups.

* Strong writing skills, competency in Microsoft Office including Excel, and experience with budget planning. 

* International travel experience.

Specific responsibilities include: 
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

* Organize tours to Norway and other countries: Work with tourism organizations and Vesterheim contacts to plan two to three tours to Norway each year. Brainstorm tour themes, educational components, itineraries, and budgets together with an annual strategic planning group that includes the Director of Development and President/CEO. Arrange logistics with travel agents, transportation organizations, hotels, attractions, artists, etc. 

* Implement the tour program: Ensure that each of the tours is uniquely connected to Vesterheim through the materials the participants receive, the educational content presented by the tour leaders, and the follow-up activities. Provide support and training for tour leaders as needed. Maintain databases and manage correspondence with all tour prospects and participants. International travel with tour groups is required.

* Promote Vesterheim’s mission through the tour program: Work with Vesterheim's marketing team to promote tours through publications, advertising, and events.

* Evaluate the tour program: Create and implement an evaluation of each tour. Work with Vesterheim's development team to follow-up with tour participants and continue their connection to Vesterheim through meaningful communication and activities.
