This individual will provide full-time (40 hours/week) support for the museum’s exhibitions program, including planning, design, fabrication, installation, and maintenance.   

Reports to 
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

Chief Curator

Minimum requirements 
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

Bachelor’s degree in art, history, archaeology, anthropology, museum studies, or closely related field and two years of museum exhibitions-related experience. Documented skill as a photographer, particularly of 3-D objects is also highly desired. 

Desired Qualifications
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

Master’s degree in art, history, archaeology, anthropology, museum studies or closely related field as well as documented experience in designing, fabricating, and installing exhibitions of 800 sq. ft. or larger. Documented skill as a photographer, particularly of 3-D objects is also highly desired. 
  
Job Duties
-----------------
{: .heading--black--large }

This position will serve as the project manager for Vesterheim’s exhibition design, fabrication, and installation process. These duties will also include planning, budgeting, and scheduling of exhibitions at Vesterheim as well as traveling exhibitions.
