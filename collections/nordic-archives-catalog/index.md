Vesterheim Archives 
=============================
Vesterheim Museum has an archive of historic photographs and documents related to Norwegian-American history and culture.  

Vesterheim shares an online catalog of its archival holdings with Luther College, called _Nordic._  To search the catalog, follow this [link](http://nordic.luther.edu) to the Luther College website.
