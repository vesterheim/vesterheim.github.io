MN 10th Inf Co E. Residence: Bath Township, Freeborn County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 27. Enrolled 15 Aug 1862. Mustered 13 Oct 1862. Private. Died 28 Feb 1865 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin, while on his way home. Sources: (MINN p475) (MCIW p479) (FCHS)
