WI 15th Inf Regimental Staff. Residence: Racine, Racine County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Commissioned 10 Dec 1862. Mustered 19 Dec 1862 at Madison, Wisconsin. Assistant Surgeon. Became ill in Nashville, Tennessee, on 27 Jan 1863. Given leave of 20 days on 12 Feb 1863. Resigned 2 Mar 1863. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 78-1; red book vol 20 p2) (Buslett p330)
