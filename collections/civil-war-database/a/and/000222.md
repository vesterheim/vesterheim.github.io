WI 3rd Inf Co F. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 17. Blue eyes, brown hair, florid complexion, 6&#146;. Enrolled 5 Oct 1864 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin, for one year. Mustered the same day. Substitute for John McKena of Moscow, Iowa County, Wisconsin. Private. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 boxes 19, 12-7)
