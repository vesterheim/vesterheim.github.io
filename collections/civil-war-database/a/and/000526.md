WI 31st Inf Co C. Residence: Dodgeville, Iowa County, Wisconsin. Born &quot;Ole Anderson Skindingsrud&quot; in Hedalen, Valders, Norway. Civil War: Age 30. Married. Enlisted for three years on 14 Aug 1862 at Dodgeville. Mustered 9 Oct 1862 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin. Private. Mustered out 20 Jun 1865 near Louisville, Kentucky. Post war: He lived in Dows, Iowa. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 145-14; red book vol 36) (Ulvestad p269) (Letter to Minneapolis Norwegian Language Newspaper, 1892, signed A.K.Brenden, Bend, Oregon)
