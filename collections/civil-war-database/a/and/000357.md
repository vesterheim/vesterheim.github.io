WI 19th Inf Co B. Residence: La Crosse, La Crosse County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Age 21. Unmarried. Enlisted for three years on 11 Jan 1862 at La Crosse. Mustered 4 Mar 1862 at Racine, Wisconsin. Private. Died of disease on 16 Jul 1862 at Norfolk, Virginia. Ole G. Hougan wrote a year later that Anderson was the first man of his company to die. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 97-17) (Ole G.
