WI 36th Inf Co H. Residence: Utica, Crawford County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Wyalusing, Grant County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 38. Hazel eyes, brown hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 29 Feb 1864 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin. Mustered 14 Mar 1864 at Madison, Wisconsin. Private. Became a prisoner of war on 25 Aug 1864 at Reams Station, Virginia. Post war: A veteran of this name, living in Red Wing, Goodhue County, Minnesota, was granted a pension of $12 a month because of debility of his heart as an after effect of scurvy. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 162-10; red book vol 41) (Pensioners on the Roll as of January 1, 1883, living in Minnesota, publ. 1994, Park Genealogical Books, Brooklyn Park, Minnesota)
