WI 11th Inf Co F. Residence: Liberty, Manitowoc County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 18. Unmarried. Farmer. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;7&#148;. Drafted. Enrolled for one year on 29 Mar 1865 at Green Bay, Wisconsin, and mustered there the same day. Private. Joined this company on 21 May 1865. Sources: (WHS red book vol 16 p116)
