WI 3rd Inf Co G. Residence: Moscow, Iowa County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 44. Blue eyes, sandy hair, sandy complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Drafted. Enrolled for one year on 28 Sep 1864 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin. Mustered the same day. Private. Died of disease in general hospital at Bridgeport, Alabama, on 2 Feb 1865. Sources: (WHS red book vol 9 p142) (Love p1061)
