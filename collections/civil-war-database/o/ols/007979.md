WI 15th Inf Co G. Residence: Coon Prairie, Bad Ax County, Wisconsin. Born 18 Jan 1829 in &Oslash;iers, Gudbrandsdalen, Norway. Came to Coon Prairie in 1852. Married on 20 May 1857 to Marie Halvorsdatter Brunsdale. Ten children. Civil War: Age 32. Married. Farmer. Gray eyes, brown hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;6&frac12;&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 6 Dec 1861 at Coon Prairie and mustered at Madison, Wisconsin, 9 Dec 1861. Private. On home leave at Camp Randall 28 Feb 1862, and did not return. Was listed as a deserter, but he rejoined his Company at Strawberry Plains, Virginia, 1 Apr 1864, and was sent to a hospital. A military court took away all pay and fees up to 1 Jan 1864. Transferred to Company H at Whiteside, Tennessee. Temporarily assigned to the Wisconsin 24th Infantry on 13 Feb 1865. Sick in Huntsville, Alabama, 13 Mar 1865. Transferred to WI 13th Inf Co I on 10 Jun 1865; however, he was reported sick in Madison, Wisconsin, 11 Jun 1865. Sick when mustered out. Post war: Buried in Coon Prairie, Wisconsin; stone reads &#147;d. 19 Mar 1881, aged 52 y 1 m 1 d&#148;.
