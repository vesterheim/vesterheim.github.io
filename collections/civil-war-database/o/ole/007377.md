WI Lt Arty 4th Battery. Residence: Beloit, Wisconsin. Civil War: Enlisted for three years on 12 Sep 1861. Private. On 31 Oct 1863, he was sick in the U. S. general Hospital in Hampton, Virginia. On 24 Mar 1864, he returned from his re-enlistment furlough. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 233-8,9,11)
