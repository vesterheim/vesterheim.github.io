WI 1st Inf Co F. Residence: Chisago Lake, Wisconsin. Civil War: Age 36. Enlisted for three years on 26 Aug 1861 at St. Croix Falls, Wisconsin. Mustered in October 1861 at Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Private. Discharged from the service 20 Feb 1863 at Louisville, Kentucky. Sources: (WHS red book vol 7)
