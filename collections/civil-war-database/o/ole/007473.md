WI 16th Inf Co K. Residence: Grafton, Ozaukee County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 19. Laborer. Unmarried. Enlisted for three years on 19 Oct 1861 at Ozaukee, Wisconsin. Private. Discharged from the service for disability on 1 Apr 1862. Sources: (WHS red book vol 21 p214)
