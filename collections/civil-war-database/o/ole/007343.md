MN 2nd Cav Co I. Residence: Winona, Winona County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 27. Enrolled 26 Nov 1863. Mustered 4 Jan 1864. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service with his company, 22 Nov 1865. Sources: (MINN p744) (MCIW p566)
