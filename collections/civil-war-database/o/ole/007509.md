WI 30th Inf Co B. Residence: Christiania, Dane County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 21. Unmarried. Gray eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 27 Oct 1863 at Madison, Wisconsin. Recruit. Mustered 27 Nov 1863 at Madison. Private. Discharged from the service for disability on 3 Jun 1864 at Milwaukee, Wisconsin. This was said to be his second enlistment; an earlier one ended in his being discharged from the service for disability (hyperactivity of the heart). Post war: In Jun 1895, he was reported living at Black River Falls, Wisconsin. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 140-3; red book vol 35) (Larsen p40)
