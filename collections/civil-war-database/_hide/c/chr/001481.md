WI 37th Inf Co K. Residence: York, Greene County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 33. Married. Blue eyes, brown hair, freckled complexion, 5&#146;6&frac12;&#148;. Drafted. Enrolled for one year on 29 Sep 1864 at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin. Mustered the same day. Private. Discharged 7 Jun 1865. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 167-13; red book vol 42)
