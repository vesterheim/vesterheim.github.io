TX 30th Cavalry Co C. CSA. Born &#147;Hendrick Olsen Dahl&#148; on 28 Nov 1827 at Hjernstad, Stange, Norway. From Hedemarken, Norway. Civil War: Enlisted for threee years on 1 Jul 1862 at Waco, McLennan County, Texas. Private. Post war: Died in Norse, Bosque County, Texas, 13 Jan 1873. Sources: (&quot;Norwegian Soldiers in Confederate Forces&quot; by C.A.Clausen &amp;amp; Derwood Johnson, publ. in Norwegian-American Studies, volume 25, Northfield, Minnesota. p114) (Ulvestad p275) (www.borgerkrigen.info) (Betty Nelson, Decorah, Iowa)
