WI 46th Inf Co C. Residence: Seneca, Crawford County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Monroe, Green County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 44. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 16 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin, and mustered there on 23 Feb 1865. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with this Company on 27 Sep 1865 at Nashville, Tennessee. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 186-4,19; red book vol 51) &#147;Torgeson, Kanut&#148;
