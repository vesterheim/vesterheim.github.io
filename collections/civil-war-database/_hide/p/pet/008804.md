MN 3rd Inf Co E. Residence: Chatfield, Fillmore County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 25. Enrolled 10 Oct 1861. Mustered 7 Nov 1861. Private. Re-enlisted 20 Dec 1863. Discharged from the service with his regiment, 2 Sep 1865. Sources: (MINN p143) (MCIW p187) (History of Fillmore County, vol 1, publ. 1912, p147)
