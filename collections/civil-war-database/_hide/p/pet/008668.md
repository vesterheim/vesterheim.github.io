WI 15th Inf Co K. Residence: Madison, Dane County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 19. Farmer. Unmarried. Brown eyes, light hair, sandy complexion, 5&#146;6&frac12;&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 3 Feb 1864 at Madison and mustered there on 17 Feb 1864. Private. Transferred to WI 13th Inf Co I on 11 Jun 1865. Sources: (WHS red book vol 18, p182)
