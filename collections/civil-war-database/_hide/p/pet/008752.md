IL 36th Inf Co K. Residence: Wayne, Du Page County, Illinois. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 33. Farmer. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 21 Sep 1861 at Wayne. Mustered at Aurora, Illinois. Private. Transferred to Veterans Reserve Corps on 17 Apr 1864. Sources: (ILSA reel three p 92)
