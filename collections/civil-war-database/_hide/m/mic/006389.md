WI 46th Inf Co G. Residence: Perry, Dane County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Clarno, Green County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 34. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 3 Feb 1865 at York, Wisconsin. Mustered 11 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Bounty $100. Private. Mustered out with this Company on 27 Sep 1865 at Nashville, Tennessee. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 186-13; red book vol 51) &#147;Mickelson, Erick&#148;
