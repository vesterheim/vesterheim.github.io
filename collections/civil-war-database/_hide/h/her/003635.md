WI 42nd Inf Co A. Residence: Scandinavia, Waupaca County, Wisconsin. Born &#147;Gjert Hermansen &Oslash;vren&aelig;s&#148; on 26 Feb 1825 at Holt, Aust Agder, Norway, son of Herman Eriksen and Ellen Svendsdatter. He came to America on the brig &#147;Juno&#148;, which sailed from Arendal, Aust Agder, 4 Jul 1849 and arrived in New York on 18 Aug 1849. He came to the Winchester settlement, Winnebago County, Wisconsin, and later moved to Iola Township, Waupaca County. He was married to Ragnild Christiansdatter &Oslash;vreb&oslash;en (1836-?). Four children. Civil War: Farmer. Age 38. Married. Gray eyes, black hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;10&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 29 Aug 1864 at Waupaca, Wisconsin. Mustered 1 Sep 1864 at Madison, Wisconsin. Bounty $100. Private. Mustered out 20 Jun 1865. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 176-2; red book vol 47) (Naeseth-Hedberg &#146;49-2601)
