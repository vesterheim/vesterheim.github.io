WI 52nd Inf Co D. Residence: Porter, Rock County, Wisconsin. Born in Drammen, Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 20. Blue eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;10&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 11 Mar 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Mustered 15 Mar 1865 at Madison. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with his company on 28 Jul 1865 at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 200-5,7,18)
