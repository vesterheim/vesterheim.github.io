WI 38th Inf Co B. Residence: Orange, Juneau County, Wisconsin. Born in Telemark, Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 38. Married. Blue eyes, brown hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;9&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 27 Aug 1864 at New Lisbon, Wisconsin. Mustered 1 Sep 1864 at Madison, Wisconsin. Recruit. Private. Bounty $100. Joined this company on 30 Sep 1864. &#147;A good soldier.&#148; Mustered out 2 Jun 1865. Buried at East Lemonweir, New Lisbon, Wisconsin. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 170-13; red book vol 43) (Wisconsin Cemetery Records) (Hedberg)
