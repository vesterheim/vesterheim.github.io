MN Mounted Rangers, 1st Regiment, Co H. Residence: Faribault, Rice County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 28. Enrolled 12 Nov 1862. Mustered 5 Dec 1862. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service with his company, 24 Nov 1863. Sources: (MINN p665) (MCIW p536)
