WI 33rd Inf Co G. Residence: Freeman, Crawford County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Age 18. Enlisted for three years on 17 Dec 1863 at Freeman. Mustered 4 Feb 1864 at Camp Randall, Madison, Wisconsin. Private. Died 21 Jan 1863 at Memphis, Tennessee. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 151-13; red book vol 38)
