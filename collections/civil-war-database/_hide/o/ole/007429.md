MN 1st Battalion Inf Co F. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 18. Enrolled 10 Mar 1865. Mustered 22 Mar 1865. Private. Discharged from the service with his company, 14 Jul 1865. Post war: In 1898, when he joined the GAR, he was living in Hayward Township, Freeborn County, Minnesota. Sources: (MINN p554) (MCIW p75) (FCHS)
