WI 3rd Inf Co F. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 40. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;11&#148;. Drafted from the Sixth district of Wisconsin. Enrolled for one year and mustered on 6 Oct 1864 at La Crosse, Wisconsin. Private. Mustered out 8 Jul 1865 at Louisville, Kentucky. Sources: (WHS red book vol 9 p128)
