WI 52nd Inf Co D. Residence: Farmington, Jefferson County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Bookbinder. Age 18. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 8 Apr 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Mustered 11 Apr 1865 at Madison. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with his company on 28 Jul 1865 at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 200-5,18)
