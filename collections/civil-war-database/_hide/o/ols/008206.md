IL 20th Inf Co F. Residence: Lockport, Will County, Illinois. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 30. Laborer. Married. Enlisted for three years on 13 Jun 1861 at Joliet, Illinois, and mustered there the same day. Private. Discharged 13 Jun 1864, his term of service having expired. Sources: (ILSA reel one p 74)
