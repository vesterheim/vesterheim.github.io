WI 34th Inf Co A. Residence: Lisbon, Waukesha County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Age 35. Unmarried. Enrolled for nine months on 17 Nov 1862 at Waukesha, Wisconsin. Mustered 5 Dec 1862 at Madison, Wisconsin. Private. Mustered out 8 Sep 1863 at Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 155-3,7; red book vol 39)
