MN 9th Inf Co H. Residence: Carver, Carver County, Minnesota. Born in Norway; military records list Sweden. Civil War: Enrolled 21 Aug 1862. Mustered 27 Oct 1862. Private. Died 8 Oct 1864 at Savannah, Georgia, while a prisoner of war. Sources: (MINN p442) (MCIW p450) (Ulvestad p304)
