IL 36th Inf Co F. Residence: Newark, Kendall County, Illinois. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 29. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;7&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 13 Aug 1861 at Newark. Mustered 20 Aug 1861 at Aurora, Illinois. Private. Died of diarrhea on 13 Sep 1862 at Cairo, Illinois. Sources: (ILSA reel three p 71)
