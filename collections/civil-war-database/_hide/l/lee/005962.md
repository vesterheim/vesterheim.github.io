WI 46th Inf Co I. Residence: York, Green County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Jefferson, Green County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 39. Married. Gray eyes, brown hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 11 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin, and mustered there on 13 Feb 1865. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with this company on 27 Sep 1865 at Nashville, Tennessee. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 186-10; red book vol 51)
