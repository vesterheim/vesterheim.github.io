MN 11th Inf Co K. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 39. Enrolled 31 Aug 1864. Mustered the same day. Private. Discharged from the service with his regiment. Mustered out 26 Jun 1865. Post war: Died 14 Sep 1889 at Otter Tail County, Minnesota. Sources: (MINN p527) (MCIW p505) (MCIW Index p8)
