WI 46th Inf Co K. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 18. Blue eyes, sandy hair, sandy complexion, 5&#146;7&frac12;&#148;. Enrolled for two years and mustered 31 Jan 1865 at La Crosse, Wisconsin, as a substitute for Horace C. Lee of Hudson City, St. Croix County, Wisconsin. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private.
