WI 3rd Inf Co \_. Born in Norway. Civil War: Painter. Age 20. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;10&#148;. Enrolled for one year on 28 Feb 1865 at La Crosse, Wisconsin. Substitute. Never joined the 3rd regiment; was kept as permanent guard in Madison, Wisconsin. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 12-2)
