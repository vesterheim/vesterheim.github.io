WI 49th Inf Co A. Residence: Mt. Horeb, Iowa County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 25. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 14 Feb 1865 at Vermont, Wisconsin. Mustered 20 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Private. Absent sick in Wisconsin on 31 Oct 1865. Post war: Buried at Mt.
