IL 23rd Inf Co K Consolidated. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Clerk. Age 31. Gray eyes, dark hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;9&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 24 Mar 1865 at Chicago, Illinois, and mustered there the same day. Bounty $100. Private. Promoted to Sergeant. Mustered out with his Company on 24 Jul 1865 at Richmond, Virginia. Sources: (ILSA reel 17 p52)
