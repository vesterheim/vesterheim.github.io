MN 2nd Inf Co K. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 21. Mustered 31 Jul 1861. Corporal. Served in Indian and Civil wars. &quot;Citizen Soldiers engaged in the Sioux Indian war of 1862&quot;. The Scandinavian Guards, organized in Nicollet County, 27 Aug 1862, and served in that county 21 days. Captain. Discharged from the service for disability. Sources: (MINN p109) (MCIW p145,807)
