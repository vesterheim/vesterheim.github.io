WI 2nd Inf Co H. Residence: Norway Township, Racine County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Shoemaker. Age 27. Unmarried. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 24 Apr 1861 at Madison. Mustered there 11 Jun 1861. Private. Promoted to Sergeant 10 May 1863. Mustered out 29 Jun 1864. Sources: (WHS red book vol 8)
