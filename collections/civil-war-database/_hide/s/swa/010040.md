WI 52nd Inf Co E. Residence: Hubbard, Dodge County, Wisconsin. Born in Telemark, Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 26. Blue eyes, red hair, light complexion, 5&#146;8&frac12;&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 14 Mar 1865 at Neenah, Wisconsin. Mustered on 29 Mar 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with his company on 28 Jul 1865 at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 200-6,19)
