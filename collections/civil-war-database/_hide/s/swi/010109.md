WI 46th Inf Co G. Residence: York, Green County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Cadiz, Green County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 42. Married. Blue eyes, brown hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;6&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 13 Feb 1865 at Monroe, Wisconsin. Mustered 23 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Bounty $100. Private. Mustered out with this Company on 27 Sep 1865 at Nashville, Tennessee. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 186-13; red book vol 51)
