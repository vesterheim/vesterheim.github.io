WI 45th Inf Co F. Residence: Primrose, Dane County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Decatur, Green County. Born in Norway. Civil War: A Soldier. Age 44. Married. Blue eyes, gray hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;10&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 1 Feb 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin, and mustered there the next day. Bounty $100. Private. Post war: Granted a pension of $8 a month because of a wound in his right shoulder. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 183-7,8; red book vol 50) (&#147;Pensioners on the Roll as of January 1, 1883 (living in Wisconsin)&#148;, Park Genealogical Books, Roseville, Minnesota, publ. 1998.)
