TX 3rd Cav Co G. CSA. Residence: Kaufman County, Texas. Born &#147;Hans Jensen Moglebustad&#148; on 13 Nov 1836 at Holt, Norway. Civil War: Private. Enlisted for one year on 3 Jun 1861 at Jefferson, Texas. Private. Wounded at Spring Hill, Tennessee, 5 Mar 1863. Sick in camp during Jul &#150; Aug 1863. Absent sick in May and Jun 1864. (One report lists him as having been killed, or died from other causes, in 1865.) Post war: Died 6 Mar 1877 in Bosque County, Texas. Sources: (&#147;Norwegian Soldiers in Confederate Forces&#148; by C.A.Clausen &amp;amp; Derwood Johnson, publ. in Norwegian-American Studies, volume 25, Northfield, Minnesota. p114) (Ulvestad p295) (www.borgerkrigen.info) &#147;Jenson, Hans&#148;
