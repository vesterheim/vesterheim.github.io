TX 31st Cav Co B. CSA. Borm Even Jensen Oddersland on 2 Apr 1843 in Tromoy, Norway. Civil War: Enlisted for the war on 2 Apr 1862 at Bosque County, Texas. Private. Promoted to Corporal in late 1863. Post war: Lived at Galveston, Texas. Known to be alive in 1880. Sources: (&quot;Norwegian Soldiers in Confederate Forces&quot; by C.A.Clausen &amp;amp; Derwood Johnson, publ. in Norwegian-American Studies, volume 25, Northfield, Minnesota. p114) (www.borgerkrigen.info) (Ulvestad p295)
