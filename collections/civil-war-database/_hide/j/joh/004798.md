IL 17th Inf Co D. Residence: Walago, Knox County, Illinois. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 36. Farmer. Blue eyes, brown hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 22 Jan 1864 at Galesburg, Illinois, and mustered on 22 Feb 1864 at Camp Butler. Recruit. Private. Transferred to IL 8th Inf Co K. Sources: (ILSA reel two p 17)
