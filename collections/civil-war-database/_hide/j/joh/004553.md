WI 25th Inf Unassigned. Residence: Coon, Vernon County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 28. Blue eyes, light hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;5&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 21 Sep 1864. Mustered the same day. Private. Transferred from the Wisconsin 17th Infantry. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 boxes 87-13,14; 121-13)
