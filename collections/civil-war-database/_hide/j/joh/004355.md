IL 23rd Inf Co K Consolidated. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 31. Hazel eyes, dark hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;1&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 27 Mar 1865 at Chicago, Illinois, and mustered there the same day. Bounty $100. Private. Mustered out with his Company on 24 Jul 1865 at Richmond, Virginia.
