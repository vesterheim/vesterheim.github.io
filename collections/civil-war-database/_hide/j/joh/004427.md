MN Hv Arty 1st Reg Co M. Residence: Carver County, Minnesota. Civil War: Age 29. Date of commission 23 Feb 1865. Captain. Enrolled 27 Feb 1865. Mustered the same day. Commander of M Company. Discharged from the service with his company, 27 Sep 1865. Sources: (MINN p569,620) (MCIW p637) (Ulvestad p255)
