MN 5th Inf Co A. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 20. Enrolled 30 Aug 1864. Mustered the same day. Recruit. Private. Wounded at battle of Nashville, Tennessee, 16 Dec 1864. Discharged from the service with his regiment, 6 Sep 1865. Sources: (MINN p239) (MCIW p285)
