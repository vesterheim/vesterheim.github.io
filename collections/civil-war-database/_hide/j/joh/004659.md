IA 25th Inf Co D. Residence: Burlington, Iowa. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 22. Enlisted 14 Aug 1862 as a Corporal. Mustered 12 Sep 1862. Wounded on 11 Jan 1863 at Arkansas Post, Arkansas. Promoted to Sergeant on 1 Jul 1864. Mustered out 6 Jun 1865 at Washington DC. Sources: (ISW-III cd p60)
