MN Mounted Rangers, 1st Regiment, Co H. Residence: Sterling, Blue Earth County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 44. Enrolled 14 Nov 1862. Mustered 5 Dec 1862. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Died 19 Oct 1863 at Fort Snelling, Minnesota. Sources: (MINN p664) (MCIW p536)
