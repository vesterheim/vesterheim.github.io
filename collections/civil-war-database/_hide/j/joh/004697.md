MN Mounted Rangers, 1st Regiment, Co M. Residence: Oakland Township, Freeborn County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 39. Enrolled 1 Nov 1862. Mustered 30 Dec 1862. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service with his company, 7 Dec 1863. Sources: (MINN p679) (MCIW p542)
