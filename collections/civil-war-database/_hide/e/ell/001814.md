WI 22nd Inf Co B. Residence: Beloit, Rock County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 22. Farmer. Unmarried. Blue eyes, light hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;8&frac12;&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 15 Aug 1862 at Beloit. Mustered 1 Sep 1862 at Racine, Wisconsin. Private. Discharged from the service for disability on 4 May 1863 at Nashville, Tennessee. Post war: Granted a pension of $12 a month because of a gunshot wound to his head. Pension #80,675. Sources: (WHS red book vol 27) (&#147;Pensioners on the Roll as of January 1, 1883 (living in Wisconsin)&#148;, Park Genealogical Books, Roseville, Minnesota, publ. 1998.)
