Residence: Wiota Township, Lafayette County, Wisconsin. Born &#147;Jon Ellevsen Gutterud&#148; on 24 Sep 1844 at Sigdal, Buskerud, Norway, son of Ellev Jonsen Gutterud and Anne Torjusdatter. He came to America with his parents and a brother, Torger, on &#147;Porsgrunn&#148;, sailing from Porsgrunn and arriving in New York 7 Jul 1848. They came to Wiota Township. Civil War: Post war: Married at Prairie du Chien, Wisconsin, in 1868 to Mary Clarquist (1845-1899). Six children. Lived in Estherville, Emmet County, Iowa. Jon died in Minneapolis, Minnesota, in 1929. Sources: (Naeseth &#146;48-530) &#147;Gutterud, Jon&#148;
