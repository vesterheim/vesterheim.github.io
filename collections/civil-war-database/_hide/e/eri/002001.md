MN Mounted Rangers, 1st Regiment, Co F. Residence not shown. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 21. Enrolled 6 Nov 1862. Mustered 24 Nov 1862. Private. Fought in both Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service for disability 21 Apr 1863 (or 3 Mar) Sources: (MINN p656) (MCIW p533)
