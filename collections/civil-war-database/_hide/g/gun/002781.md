US 785th Engineer Petroleum Co. Residence: Brooklyn, New York. Born in Grimstad, Norway, son of Olaf and Ragnar Gunnulfsen. They came to America in the 1920s, settling in Brooklyn. Second World War: He served four year in the Army in North Africa, Italy and the Philippines. Post war: Married in 1948 to Beverly Roman. Six children. Lived in Long Island, New York, until 1956 when they moved to Reading, Massachusetts. Moved to Laurel, Prince George&#146;s County, Maryland, in 1978, where he worked as a budget analyst for the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. He died 21 Jul 2004. Sources: (Obituary, Norway Times, New York, 25 Aug 2004, p 10)
