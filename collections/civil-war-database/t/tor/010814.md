WI 14th Inf Co G. Residence: Manitowoc, Manitowoc County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 31. Farmer. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, ruddy complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Drafted. Enrolled for one year and mustered 29 Dec 1864. Private. Took part in the siege of Fort Spanish, Alabama, in 1865. Sources: (WHS red book vol 19, p140)
