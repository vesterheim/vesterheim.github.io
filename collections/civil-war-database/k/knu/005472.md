WI 15th Inf Co I. Residence: Scandinavia, Waupaca County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Age 23. Unmarried. Enlisted for three years on 19 Nov 1861 at Scandinavia and mustered at Madison, Wisconsin, 20 Dec 1861. Private. Died of disease at Mound City, Illinois, 26 Oct 1862. Buried in Mound City National Cemetery; section A, grave 875.
