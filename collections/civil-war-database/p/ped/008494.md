TX 31st Cav Co B. CSA. Born &#147;Christopher Pedersen Holtet&#148; on 14 Jun 1841 at Stange, Norway. Civil War: Enlisted for the war on 2 Apr 1862 from Bosque County, Texas. Private. Promoted to Sergeant in 1863. Absent with leave from 2 Aug 1863 to 6 Feb 1864. He was sick in camp during March and April 1865; then moved to a hospital in Houston, Texas. Post war: Died 15 Sep 1930 in Bosque County [One source says he died in Grant County, Minnesota]. Sources: (&quot;Norwegian Soldiers in Confederate Forces&quot; by C.A.Clausen &amp;amp; Derwood Johnson, publ. in Norwegian-American Studies, volume 25, Northfield, Minnesota. p115) (www.borgerkrigen.info) (Ulvestad p321)
