IL 33rd Inf Co K. Residence: Chicago, Cook County, Illinois. Born in Norway. Civil War: Sailor. Age 33. Gray eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;6. Enlisted for three years on 1 Jan 1864 at Indianola, Illinois. Mustered there the same day. Private. Mustered out 24 Nov 1865 at Vicksburg, Mississippi. Sources: (ILSA reel three p 61)
