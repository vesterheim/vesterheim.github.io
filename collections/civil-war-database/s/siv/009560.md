WI 45th Inf Co B. Residence: Liberty, Manitowoc County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 34. Married. Gray eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;4&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 7 Oct 1864 at Liberty. Mustered 4 Nov 1864 at Madison, Wisconsin. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out from the Post Hospital at Nashville, Tennessee, on 20 May 1865. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 183-3; red book vol 50) &#147;Siverson, Ollan&#148;
