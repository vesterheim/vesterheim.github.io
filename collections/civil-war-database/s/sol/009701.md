WI 15th Inf Co D. Residence: Chicago, Illinois. Civil War: Age 35. Unmarried. Enlisted for three years on 30 Sep 1861 at Chicago, and mustered at Madison, Wisconsin, 15 Nov 1861. Private. Detached to Company A at Mississippi River Island No. 10 from 1 Apr to 1 May 1862. Stayed behind with the baggage train in Nashville, Tennessee, on 6 Dec 1862. Assigned as clerk in the Brigade commissary on 9 May 1863. Sick in Stevenson, Alabama, 28 Aug 1863. Transferred to the Veteran Reserve Corps on 1 Sep 1863. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 boxes 76-6, 78-3; red book vol 20 p64) (Buslett p457) (Ulvestad p329) (Ager p302)
