WI 52nd Inf Co D. Residence: Porter, Rock County, Wisconsin. Born in Kongsberg, Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 19. Brown eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 11 Mar 1865 at Madison, Wisconsin. Mustered there on 15 Mar 1865. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Mustered out with his company on 28 Jul 1865 at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 200-5,7,18)
