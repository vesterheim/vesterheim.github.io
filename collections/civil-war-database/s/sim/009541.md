IL 82nd Inf Co I. Residence: Chicago, Cook County, Illinois. Born in Kragsovie (?), Norway. Civil War: Sailmaker. Age 21. Unmarried. Blue eyes, light hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;11&#148;.
