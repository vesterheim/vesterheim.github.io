WI 22nd Inf Co G. Residence: Wiota, Lafayette County, Wisconsin. Civil War: Unmarried. Age 18. Enlisted for three years on 9 Aug 1862 at Monroe, Wisconsin. Mustered 2 Sep 1862 at Camp Utley, Racine, Wisconsin. Private. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 111-9,14; red book vol 27) [Red book lists residence as Shinner, Green County.]
