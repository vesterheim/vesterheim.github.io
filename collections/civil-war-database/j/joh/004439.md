IL 7th Cav Co L. Residence: Chicago, Cook County, Illinois. Born in Norway. Civil War: Miller. Age 22. Gray eyes, brown hair, light complexion, 5&#146;4&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 16 Jan 1865 at Chicago and mustered there the same day. Private. Mustered out 4 Nov 1865 at Nashville, Tennessee. Sources: (ILSA reel 14 p68)
