WI 4th Cav Co E. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 19. Blue eyes, light hair, light complexion, 5&#146;9&#148;. Enlisted for three years on 31 Mar 1864 at Racine, Wisconsin. Mustered 2 Apr 1864 at Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Recruit. Private. Enlistment credited to the town of Wauwatosa, Milwaukee County, Wisconsin. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 223-7)
