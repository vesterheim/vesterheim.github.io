TX McCord&#39;s Frontier Cav Co E. CSA. Civil War: Enlisted 21 Dec 1862 at Camp Salmon, Texas. Private. Member of the Settlers &quot;Home Guard&quot; in the Indian wars. Served in Texas during the Civil War. Post war: Died in Bosque County, Texas.
