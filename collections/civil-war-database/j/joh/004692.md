MN 6th Inf Co C. Residence: Meeker County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 21. Enrolled 29 Jul 1862. Mustered 3 Oct 1862. Private. Transferred to 3rd Minnesota Battery on 1 (or 3) May 1863. Discharged from the service 24 Jul 1865. Sources: (MINN p287,p802) (MCIW p334)
