WI 49th Inf Co B. Residence: Winchester, Winnebago County, Wisconsin. Born 14 Apr 1821 in Denmark. Civil War: Age 43. Married. Farmer. Blue eyes, dark hair, ruddy complexion, 5&#146;3&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 21 Feb 1865 at Green Bay, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Lawrence, Brown County, Wisconsin. Private. Furloughed 21 Aug 1865 because he was sick. Reported on 12 Sep 1865 to be &#147;sick at home&#148;. Mustered out with his company at Benton Barracks, Missouri, on 1 Nov 1865 Sources: (History of the town of Winchester, Winnebago County, Wisconsin, publ. 2002 p 4-14)
