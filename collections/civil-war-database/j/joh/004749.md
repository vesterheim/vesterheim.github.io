WI 50th Inf Co H. Residence: Clyde, Iowa County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 28. Married. Blue eyes, light hair, florid complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 10 Mar 1865 at Boscobel, Wisconsin. Mustered there the same day. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 195-9)
