MN 3rd Inf Co D. Residence: Red Wing, Goodhue County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 30. Enrolled 9 Oct 1861. Mustered 4 Nov 1861. Private. Discharged from the service for disability 12 Jul 1862. Re-enlisted 5 Feb 1864. Discharged from the service with his regiment, 2 Sep 1865. Sources: (MINN p137) (MCIW p185)
