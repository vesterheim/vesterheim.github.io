IA 32nd Inf Co D. Residence: Boonsborough, Iowa. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 34. Enlisted 5 Jan 1864. Mustered 20 Jan 1864. Private. Taken prisoner 9 Apr 1864 at Pleasant Hill, Louisiana.
