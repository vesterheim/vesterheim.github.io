MN Brackett&#39;s Cavalry Battalion Co B. Residence: Jackson, Jackson County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 18. Enrolled 23 Oct 1861. Mustered the same day. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Re-enlisted 1 Jan 1864. Discharged from the service with his company, 1 Jun 1866. Sources: (MINN p692) (MCIW p588)
