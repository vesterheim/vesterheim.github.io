MN Mounted Rangers, 1st Regiment, Co B. Civil War: Residence: Oshwa, Nicollet County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 24. Enrolled 11 Oct 1862. Mustered 29 Oct 1862. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service for disability, 29 Mar 1863. Sources: (MINN p641) (MCIW p528)
