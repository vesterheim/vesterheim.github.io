WI 45th Inf Co F. Residence: Grafton, Ozaukie County, Wisconsin. Enlistment credited to Theresa, Dodge County. Born in Norway. Civil War: Farmer. Age 28. Married. Blue eyes, dark brown hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;10&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 16 Jan 1865 at Fond du Lac, Wisconsin, and mustered there the same day. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 183-8; red book vol 50)
