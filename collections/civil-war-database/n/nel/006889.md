MN 2nd Cav Co I. Residence: Winona, Winona County, Minnesota. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 29. Enrolled 21 Nov 1863. Mustered 4 Jan 1864. Private. Served in Indian and Civil wars. Discharged from the service 3 Dec 1864 at Fort Ridgely, Minnesota. Sources: (MINN p744) (MCIW p566)
