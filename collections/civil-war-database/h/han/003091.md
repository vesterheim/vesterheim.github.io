MN 7th Inf. Co H. Residence: Henderson, Minnesota. Born in Germany. Civil War: Age 26. Enrolled 13 Aug 1862. Mustered 8 Oct 1862. Corporal. Served in both Indian and Civil wars. Promoted to Sergeant, 8 Dec 1864. Discharged from the service with his regiment on 16 Aug 1865. Sources: (MINN p351) (MCIW p382) (Ulvestad p259)
