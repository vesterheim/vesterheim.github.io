US Army 113th F. S. Battalion, Co C. Born 7 Jul 1886 at Christiania, Norway. First World War: Age 32. Entered the service in May 1918 at Camp Dodge, Iowa. Private. Served in France. Discharged in Jun 1919 at Camp Dodge. Post war: Died at age 43 on 9 May 1929 at Rapid City, South Dakota, when he was accidentally caught between a tractor and a disc hitch. Buried in Faith, South Dakota, Cemetery, lot 10, block 71, grave 1. Sources: (SDSHS WPA Veterans Cemetery Records, Meade County)
