IL 26th Inf Co B. Residence: Freeport, Stephenson County, Illinois. Born in Denmark. Civil War: Age 37. Farmer. Unmarried. Gray eyes, sandy hair, sandy complexion, 6&#146;. Enlisted for three years on 15 Aug 1861 at Freeport and mustered that day at Camp Butler. Private. Discharged for disability on 12 Jul 1862 at Danville, Mississippi. Sources: (ILSA reel two p 53)
