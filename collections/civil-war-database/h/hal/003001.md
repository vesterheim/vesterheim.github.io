WI 16th Inf Co G. Residence: Deerfield, Dane County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Age 35. Farmer. Married. Blue eyes, brown hair, fair complexion, 5&#146;8&frac34;&#148;. Drafted. Enrolled for one year on 19 Sep 1864 at Janesville, Wisconsin, and mustered there the same day. Private. Joined this company on 8 Nov 1864. Was on the march through Georgia, the Carolina, Virginia and Washington, DC. Discharged from the service 5 Jun 1865 at Washington, DC. Sources: (WHS red book vol 21 p154)
