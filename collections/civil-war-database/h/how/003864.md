US Engineer Regiment of the West, Co K. Residence: Burlington, Iowa. Born in Sweden. Civil War: Age 30. Enlisted 28 Sep 1861. Mustered 31 Oct 1861. Private. Re-enlisted and re-mustered 15 Feb 1864.
