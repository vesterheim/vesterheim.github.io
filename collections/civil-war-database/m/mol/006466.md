US Army. 33rd Engineers, Co B. Residence: Minneapolis, Minnesota. Born 5 Oct 1895 in Trelleborg, Sweden. First World War: Enlisted 27 Feb 1918 at Minneapolis. Private. Discharged 10 Jun 1919 at Camp Grant, Illinois. Post war: Died 20 Dec 1936 at Fort Snelling, Minnesota, of a perforated ulcer. Buried in Riverside Cemetery, Huron, South Dakota. Sources: (SDSHS, WPA Veterans Graves Registration, Beadle County)
