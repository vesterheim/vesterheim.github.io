WI 50th Inf Co K. Residence: Freeman, Crawford County, Wisconsin. Born in Norway. Civil War: Tinner. Age 26. Unmarried. Hazel eyes, brown hair, dark complexion, 5&#146;8&#148;. Enlisted for one year on 6 Apr 1865 at Boscobel, Wisconsin. Mustered there the same day. Bounty $100, $33.33 paid. Private. On 31 Dec 1865, he is listed as having been left sick at Fort Randall, Dakota Territory, since 15 Sep 1865. Sources: (WHS Series 1200 box 195-11,17)
