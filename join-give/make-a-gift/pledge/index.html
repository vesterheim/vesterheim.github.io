Pledge Now

Pledging a gift to Vesterheim allows you to stretch your payments over a period of months or years.

To pledge your donation to Vesterheim, please print a pledge form (pdf) and send the form, along with your check or credit card information to:
Development Department
Vesterheim Museum
P.O. Box 379
Decorah, IA 52101

For questions about pledging a gift to Vesterheim, contact Steve Grinna at 563-382-9681, ext. 109, or email at sgrinna@vesterheim.org.
