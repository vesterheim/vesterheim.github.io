Patti Goke of Saint Cloud, Minnesota, has been rosemaling for over 20 years and has taken many classes with American and Norwegian artists. One of her rosemaling experiences was painting in the Raftevold Hotel in Hornindal, Nordfjord, Norway. Patti is a Vesterheim Gold Medalist, Vesterheim *Rosemaling Letter* editor, and contributor of rosemaling articles and projects for *Art Apprentice Online* e-zine.
