Jan Mostrom, from Chanhassen, Minnesota, earned her Vesterheim Gold Medal in 1999. She learned to weave from Lila Nelson while at Luther College and since then has been weaving in the Nordic tradition. She has taken weaving instruction from many Norwegian teachers here and in Norway. She teaches weaving and rug hooking in the Midwest. Her work was included in _Norwegian Folk Art: The Migration of a Tradition_, the traveling exhibition curated by Marion Nelson, former Vesterheim Executive Director.
