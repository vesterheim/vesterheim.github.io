Torgeir Lirhus

Torgeir Lirhus is from Skulestadmo near Voss, Norway. He received his training at Norsk Kunsthandverksskole with instructors Ven Valevatn and Olav N. Dolve. Torgeir has been working with wood as a carpenter and folk artist for over 30 years, and has been a teacher for 20 years at Voss Folkeh√∏gskole. He taught woodcarving at Vesterheim in 2002 and 2005 and in Voss for the folk art tour to Norway in 2010.
