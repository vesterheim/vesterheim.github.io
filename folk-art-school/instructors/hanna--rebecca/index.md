Rebecca Hanna is Scandinavian American, but only began pursuing her heritage as a student at Luther college. She is an elementary-school teacher at South Winneshiek School, and is very active in the Decorah Sons of Norway chapter. Rebecca is a Vesterheim Gold Medalist and was instrumental in the creation of _Vesterheim_ magazine, Vol. 3, No. 2, dedicated to woodworking.
