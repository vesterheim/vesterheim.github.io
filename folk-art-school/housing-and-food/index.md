Housing
-------------
Students must make their own housing and dining arrangements.

The Winneshiek County Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau has information about housing [here](http://www.visitdecorah.com/accommodations).

Luther College also maintains an extensive up-to-date list of area housing options [here](http://www.luther.edu/decorah/lodging/). 

If you have trouble finding housing, please call Vesterheim at (563) 382-9681 for assistance. There may be other options for accommodations in the area, including private housing.

Food
-------------
A variety of moderately priced restaurants are within walking distance from Vesterheim. The Winneshiek County Convention &amp; Visitors Bureau has information about area restaurants [here](http://www.visitdecorah.com/restaurants). 

The Westby-Torgerson Education Center has a student lounge located on the third floor. Facilities include a refrigerator, microwave, stove, sink, coffee maker, pop machine, and tables and chairs.

There are also tables outside the museum's Main Building and there are many near-by parks for picnics.
